# Billing and Invoices

Summary: How customers view invoices, update payment methods, and request refunds.

Latest Update: Self-serve refunds are now available for charges under $50.

## Common Tasks
- View invoices: Account → Billing → Invoices
- Update payment method: Account → Billing → Payment
- Request a refund: contact support or use self-serve for charges under $50
