# Password Reset Flow

Summary: Steps to help a customer who is locked out or cannot reset their password.

Latest Update: Reset links now expire after 30 minutes (was 24 hours).

## Steps
1. Confirm the account email.
2. Ask the customer to use the "Forgot password" link on the login page.
3. Reset links expire after 30 minutes — resend if needed.
4. If the account is locked after 5 failed attempts, it auto-unlocks in 15 minutes.
